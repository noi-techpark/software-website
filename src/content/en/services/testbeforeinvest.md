Access to professional engineering expertise and testing infrastructure for the collaborative development and experimentation of R&D prototypes across strategic digital technology domains.

The Test Before Invest service provides organisations with structured access to dedicated engineering support and experimentation infrastructure. This enables the initial development and rigorous technical validation of R&D prototypes prior to major investment decisions. By offering a controlled, professional environment, the service supports evidence based development and reduces the financial and technical risk associated with early stage technology adoption.

<!--more--> 

**Domains of Application**

**Artificial Intelligence:**	
Development, training, benchmarking, and performance evaluation of AI models and machine learning pipelines under realistic operational conditions.

**Internet of Things:**	
Prototyping and comprehensive validation of connected device architectures, communication protocols, and IoT system integration scenarios.

**Data:**	
Engineering and stress testing of data pipelines, storage architectures, and analytics applications at representative scale.

**Cybersecurity:**
Controlled execution of penetration testing, vulnerability assessments, and threat simulation exercises in a secure, isolated environment.

**Digital Sovereignty:**	
Development and evaluation of Open Source and vendor independent technology stacks to support strategic autonomy and regulatory compliance objectives.
