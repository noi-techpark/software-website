Access to professional testing infrastructure for R&D prototype experimentation across strategic digital technology domains.

The Test Before Invest service provides organisations with structured access to dedicated testing and experimentation infrastructure, enabling the rigorous technical validation of R&D prototypes prior to full-scale investment decisions. By offering a controlled, professional-grade environment, the service supports evidence-based development and reduces the financial and technical risk associated with early-stage technology adoption.

<!--more--> 

**Domains of Application**

**Artificial Intelligence:**	
Training, benchmarking, and performance evaluation of AI models and machine learning pipelines under realistic operational conditions.

**Internet of Things:**	
End-to-end validation of connected device architectures, communication protocols, and IoT system integration scenarios.

**Data:**	
Prototyping and stress-testing of data pipelines, storage architectures, and analytics applications at representative scale.

**Cybersecurity:**
Controlled execution of penetration testing, vulnerability assessments, and threat simulation exercises in a secure, isolated environment.

**Digital Sovereignty:**	
Evaluation of open-source and vendor-independent technology stacks to support strategic autonomy and regulatory compliance objectives.
